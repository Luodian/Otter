The following are the characteristics of Himedaka's appearance.

- It reaches a maximum size of about 4 cm (1.6 in).
- Himedaka comes in various colors, including red, orange, yellow, and white.
- The body is elongated and slightly compressed laterally.
- It has a single dorsal fin, a rounded caudal fin, and a pair of pectoral and pelvic fins.
- The eyes are relatively large and positioned laterally on the head.
- The mouth is small and terminal, meaning it is located at the front of the head.
- Himedaka has small, thin, and translucent scales.
- Males are generally more colorful and have longer fins than females
